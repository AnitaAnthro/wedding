Ceremony & Reception

Mt. Woodson Castle

The Ceremony & Reception will be held at Mt. Woodson Castle in Ramona, CA. This Historical Castle was built in 1921 as a private residence for Amy Strong, a famous San Diego dress designer. The Strong home in Ramona is the vision of this artistic woman, the talents of her architects, and the philosophy of the Craftsman Movement. Learn More...
